A World Map vision of VR (Virtual Reality) importance in the last five years:

· Conclusions at December of 2016

- VR is growing in all the World in the last year.
- PlayStation4 and Xbox 360 are more relevant than VR.
- The growing of PlayStation4 and Xbox is influenced by VR.
- VR is more relevant in Europe, North America, Canada and Australia.

· Conclusions at April of 2017

- The situation is making tendencies.
- The tendence of Playstation 4 is growing.
- And the tendence of Xbox 360 and Virtual Reality are decreasing. Perhaps, Virtual Reality is a transient trend and XBox 360 have to change their Game Engine.
